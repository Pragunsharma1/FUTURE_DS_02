# 📊 Customer Churn & Risk Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as **Internship Task 2 at Future Interns**. The objective was to analyze customer churn behavior and identify high-risk customer segments using interactive Power BI dashboards.

The dashboards provide valuable insights into customer retention, service usage patterns, contract preferences, payment methods, and revenue impact. The analysis helps businesses make data-driven decisions to reduce churn and improve customer loyalty.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

Customer churn is one of the biggest challenges for subscription-based businesses. Losing customers directly impacts revenue and profitability.

This project aims to:

* Identify factors contributing to customer churn.
* Analyze customer risk segments.
* Discover patterns in customer behavior.
* Provide actionable recommendations for improving retention.

## 📈 Dashboard 1: Customer Churn Analysis

### Key KPIs

* Customers at Risk
* Tech Tickets
* Admin Tickets
* Yearly Charges
* Monthly Charges

### Analysis Areas

* Customer Demographics
* Subscription Tenure
* Service Usage
* Contract Types
* Payment Methods
* Internet Service Distribution

### Key Findings

#### Customer Demographics

* Churn is nearly equally distributed between male and female customers.
* 25% of churned customers are senior citizens.
* Customers without dependents are more likely to churn.

#### Subscription Tenure

* Over 55% of churned customers leave within the first year.
* Customer loyalty increases with tenure.

#### Contract Analysis

* Month-to-month customers show the highest churn rate.
* Customers with long-term contracts are significantly less likely to leave.

#### Service Insights

* Fiber Optic users contribute heavily to churn.
* Customers using Tech Support and Online Security services demonstrate lower churn tendencies.

#### Payment Analysis

* Electronic Check users represent the highest churn segment.

## 📊 Dashboard 2: Customer Risk Analysis

### Key KPIs

* Total Customers: 7,043
* Churn Rate: 26.54%
* Yearly Charges: $16.06M
* Admin Tickets: 3,632
* Tech Tickets: 2,955

### Analysis Areas

* Churn by Internet Service
* Contract Analysis
* Customer Tenure
* Payment Methods
* Revenue Contribution

### Key Findings

#### Internet Service Analysis

* Fiber Optic customers have the highest churn rate (41.89%).
* Fiber Optic customers generate the highest revenue but also carry the highest risk.

#### Contract Analysis

* Month-to-month contracts have a churn rate of 42.71%.
* Two-year contracts show the lowest churn rate at 2.83%.

#### Tenure Analysis

* Nearly half of all churn occurs within the first year.

#### Payment Method Analysis

* Electronic Check users experience the highest churn rate (45.29%).
* Auto-payment methods demonstrate better customer retention.

---

## 💡 Business Recommendations

### 1. Improve Customer Onboarding

Focus on engaging customers during their first year to reduce early churn.

### 2. Promote Long-Term Contracts

Offer incentives for annual and multi-year plans.

### 3. Address Fiber Optic Customer Concerns

Investigate service quality, pricing, and customer satisfaction issues.

### 4. Encourage AutoPay Adoption

Promote bank transfer and credit card payment methods.

### 5. Increase Service Adoption

Encourage customers to use:

* Tech Support
* Online Security
* Device Protection

These services may improve customer retention and satisfaction.
